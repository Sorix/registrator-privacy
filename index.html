Privacy Policy

App: Registrator
Android package: lizaalert.registrator
Last updated: 12 May 2026

Registrator is a local Android application. The app does not send user data to the developer's servers or to third parties.

Camera Access

The app requests camera access only to scan QR codes inside the app. The app does not save photos or videos from the camera. Camera data is processed locally on the device and is not sent to any server.

Local Data

The app may store application data locally on the user's device. This data is not transmitted to the developer or to third parties. Users can delete local app data by deleting the app or clearing app storage in Android system settings.

Network, Analytics, and Advertising

The app does not use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or third-party tracking SDKs. The app does not use the internet to transfer user data.

Data Sharing

The app does not sell, share, or transfer user data to third parties.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through GitHub Issues: https://github.com/Sorix/registrator-privacy/issues.
